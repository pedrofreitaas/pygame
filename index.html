The meteor is cast with a delay before landing, but upon impact, it explodes, causing damage in a certain area.

This is a demonstration of the hookax power:

The hookax has to ability to pull the player and to be pulled by the player. It can stick to a entity or a surface.

Defend, attack, slide and running animations:

Both attacks and slides can be redirected by player's movement.

Sprint power

The Sprint power increases the player's general speed. Making actions happen faster, attacks reach further and other funny things.
